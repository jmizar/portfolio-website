About me

My Story

I have always had a sheer fascination for new technologies and how innovative products could shape our lifestyle. In college, I dived headfirst into engineering as I felt that would eventually place me in the midst of all the new technological wonders that were coming.

During my degree, and my first professional roles I was trained into analysing and solving situations from an objective perspective. Moulding my mind into procuring the most efficient way to approach problems.

However, I was never able to shake the feeling that I also wanted to create, design and build visually appealing content. In order to quench that thirst I embarked myself into different design projects until I eventually discovered UX.

The rest I guess, it is a simple story, I fell in love with the user experience world and I haven't stopped learning since. A thrilling journey for someone with a mind for logical thinking but a heart for creative and unconventional solutions.
